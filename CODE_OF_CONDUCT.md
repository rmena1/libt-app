# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming and respectful place for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Give and receive feedback with care.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors whose behavior is harmful to the project or its community.
